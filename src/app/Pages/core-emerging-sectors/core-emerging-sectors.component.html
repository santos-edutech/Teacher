Job Mapping in Core Emerging Sectors

Forecasting the futuristic trends of Industry

Countries around world are creating wealth through innovation and technology. In the era of automation, Industry 4.0, Data Science, ML/AI it is very important for students to keep a track of the rapid changes happening in each industry sector, this is pivotal to learn the right skills and choose the relevant industry paths to build a successful career. The core of EDUCTECHEX programs is a blend of navigated learning coupled with forecast from industry players in terms of tech innovation, skill set building. This model is intended to provide students with a practical model to gain access to jobs in cutting edge technologies in industry sectors.
